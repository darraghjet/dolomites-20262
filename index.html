01 · The Expedition

A hiking expedition with a little Italy on the side

The full Alta Via 1 is a north-to-south crossing of the Italian Dolomites — a UNESCO World Heritage landscape of pale limestone towers that turn gold and pink at sunrise and sunset. Ten days walking through the heart of them, sleeping in a different mountain refugio every night, carrying only a light daily pack. Most visitors see the Dolomites from a car window. This is the full traverse.

💶 Budget snapshot

Per person, excluding airfare · target ~$3,500

  • Refugio half board ×10: $900–1,200
  • Venice hotels ×2 nights: $300–600
  • Trains & buses: $75–150
  • Meals outside refugios: $150–250
  • Gear, insurance, incidentals: $350–850

🎒 The ten rules

  • Protect Lagazuoi — book it first, guard it
  • Protect Nuvolau — book immediately after
  • Stay for the Lagazuoi sunrise and sunset
  • Travel light — 18–22 lbs, trail runners
  • Take photos together, not 500 mountain shots
  • Celebrate the finish properly
